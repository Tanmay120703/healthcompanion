What It Is

A muscle gain diet emphasizes building lean muscle through strength training and a calorie surplus with high-protein, nutrient-dense foods.

Macronutrients and Their Role in Muscle Gain

  • Calories: To build muscle, consume more calories than you burn (caloric surplus). These excess calories provide the energy needed for muscle synthesis.
  • Proteins: Crucial for muscle repair and growth. Aim for 1.6‚Äì2.2g protein per kg body weight.
  • Fats: Support hormone production, including testosterone. Include healthy fats like nuts, ghee, and seeds.
  • Carbohydrates: Fuel workouts and aid in muscle recovery. Opt for complex carbs like sweet potatoes and brown rice.
